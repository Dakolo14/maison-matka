About Us

Maison Matka is more than property management; it is a house of journeys, rooted in Slavic strength and French refinement. Maison means "house," Matka means "mother", together they embody stability, care, and belonging.

We exist to bring discipline and hospitality into perfect balance. For landlords and investors, that means rigorous systems, transparent reporting, and long-term value. For residents and guests, it means intentional spaces that feel warm, cared for, and truly welcoming.

At Maison Matka, luxury is not excess. It is trust. It is stability. It is the quiet assurance that you are looked after, whether you own the home or call it your own for a while.
