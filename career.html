Investing in You

When you join Maison Matka, you're joining a private, family led firm with a rich history of transformative work in our communities. You're joining a company with a pioneering focus on sustainability, innovation and placemaking.

"Maison Matka means strength, care, and belonging. These values allow us to balance precision with hospitality — creating spaces defined by trust, stability, and genuine warmth."

Why Work at Maison Matka?

Join a team redefining property management, where precision meets care, and every role contributes to creating spaces that feel like home.

Pursuit of Excellence

Be part of a company that values discipline, refinement, and the highest standards in everything we do.

Empowering Leadership

Work with leaders who see your strengths, guide with purpose, and help you grow with confidence.

Culture of Care

Experience a workplace built on respect, balance, and genuine connection, where people come first.

Meaningful Growth

Develop your skills, expand your career, and contribute to shaping the future of luxury property management.

What We're Looking For

We seek individuals who embody discipline, hospitality, and a genuine commitment to excellence. Whether you're experienced in property management or eager to learn, we value integrity, attention to detail, and the ability to create meaningful connections with clients and residents alike.

Benefits

What You'll Gain

Join Maison Matka and gain more than a job — gain a purpose. Enjoy competitive compensation, professional development opportunities, a supportive team culture, and the chance to shape the future of luxury property management in Alberta. Your growth is our priority.
