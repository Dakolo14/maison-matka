Frequently asked questions

How can Maison Matka help me buy a home?

Maison Matka offers expert guidance, market insights, and a curated selection of properties to help you find your dream home with ease. We handle the negotiations and paperwork so you can focus on moving in.

What areas do you cover in your listings?

We specialize in the Greater Vancouver area, including Downtown, West Vancouver, North Vancouver, and Kitsilano, focusing on luxury residences and high-potential investment properties.

Do you assist with property valuation?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive comparative market analysis (CMA) to ensure your property is priced competitively to attract the right buyers while maximizing your return.

How do I start the selling process?

Starting is simple. Contact us through our website or give us a call to schedule a consultation. We will visit your property, discuss your goals, and outline a tailored strategy for your sale.

Can you help with investment properties?

Yes, our team has extensive experience in real estate investment. We can help you identify properties with high rental yield potential or long-term appreciation value, and guide you through the specifics of investment financing.
